About EatClever

EatClever is a free, open nutrition knowledge platform designed to help people make informed food choices at every stage of life.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding what you eat is the first step toward better health. EatClever makes nutrition science accessible, practical, and engaging for everyone ‚Äî no jargon, no fad diets, just evidence-based guidance.

Our Approach

Every recommendation on EatClever is grounded in data from leading health organizations and peer-reviewed research. We focus on whole foods, balanced eating, and practical advice that fits real life.

Data Sources

Our nutrition data comes from trusted sources including the USDA FoodData Central, WHO Nutrition Guidelines, NIH Office of Dietary Supplements, and the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. See our full methodology for details.
